# 3. Narrative Engine

## Story Structure: Claim → Analysis → Verdict
*   **The Claim (0:00-1:00):** Present popular belief/myth
*   **Why People Believe It (1:00-3:00):** Psychology of the myth
*   **The Evidence (3:00-9:00):** Systematic breakdown
*   **The Verdict (9:00-11:00):** Definitive conclusion
*   **The Real Story (11:00-12:00):** What's actually true

## Episode Arc Pattern
*   Start by validating the myth's appeal
*   Gradually introduce doubt
*   Present undeniable evidence
*   Satisfying "aha" moment

## Series Arc Potential
*   Vertical series: "Tech Myths Debunked", "History Myths", etc.
*   Seasonal: "The Most Viral Lies of 2025"

## Character Role Definitions
*   **The Skeptic (narrator):** Friendly but firm
*   **The Believer (straw man):** Represents common misconception
*   **The Evidence (visual demonstrations):** Star of the show
