# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 50-65 hours
*   **Research:** 20 hours (must be airtight)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 10 hours
*   **Storyboarding:** 6 hours
*   **Illustration:** 10 hours (diagrams complex)
*   **Animation:** 12 hours
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 6 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements
*   Scientific diagram templates
*   Comparison split-screen templates
*   "Myth/Fact" graphics
*   Experiment animation rigs

## Reusable Template Elements
*   Opening "myth presentation" card
*   Evidence checklist
*   Verdict stamp
*   Fact correction overlay

## Team Composition Needed
*   **Researcher (with expertise)**
*   **Writer**
*   **Technical illustrator**
*   **Animator**
*   **Voiceover**
