# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 60-80 hours
*   **Research:** 25 hours (accuracy critical)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 12 hours (clarity is hard)
*   **Storyboarding:** 8 hours (visual flow complex)
*   **Illustration:** 15 hours
*   **Animation:** 15 hours
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 8 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements
*   Flowchart libraries
*   Diagram templates
*   System visualization tools
*   Icon libraries

## Reusable Template Elements
*   Opening "system view" zoom
*   Decision node templates
*   User journey paths
*   Consequence cascade animations

## Team Composition Needed
*   **Systems analyst/Researcher**
*   **Technical writer**
*   **Motion graphics designer**
*   **Voiceover**
