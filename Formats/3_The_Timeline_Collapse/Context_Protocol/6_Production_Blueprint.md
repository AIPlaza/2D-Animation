# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 45-55 hours
*   **Research:** 18 hours (timeline must be accurate)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 10 hours (pacing critical)
*   **Storyboarding:** 5 hours
*   **Illustration:** 8 hours
*   **Animation:** 10 hours
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 8 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements
*   Clock/timer templates
*   Event ticker system
*   Map templates (if geographic)
*   Transition effects (fast cuts)

## Reusable Template Elements
*   Digital clock overlay
*   Timeline scrubber
*   Event pop-ups
*   Breaking news graphics

## Team Composition Needed
*   **Solo:** Possible with templates
*   **Optimal:**
    *   Researcher
    *   Motion designer
    *   Editor
    *   Voiceover
