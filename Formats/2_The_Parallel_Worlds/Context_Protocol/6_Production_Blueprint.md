# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 50-70 hours
*   **Research:** 20 hours (two subjects = double work)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 10 hours (parallel narrative complex)
*   **Storyboarding:** 6 hours
*   **Illustration:** 12 hours (mirror assets)
*   **Animation:** 12 hours
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 6 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements
*   Split-screen composition templates
*   Mirrored character designs
*   Timeline visualization system
*   Decision tree diagrams

## Reusable Template Elements
*   Split-screen frame
*   Timeline scrubber (dual-track)
*   "VS" intro graphic
*   Outcome comparison cards
*   Decision point lightning effect

## Team Composition Needed
*   **Solo:** Challenging (parallel research heavy)
*   **Optimal:**
    *   Researcher/Writer
    *   Vector artist
    *   Motion designer
    *   Voiceover
