# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 55-75 hours
*   **Research:** 20 hours (scenario development)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 10 hours (hypothetical narratives)
*   **Storyboarding:** 8 hours
*   **Illustration:** 15 hours (model design)
*   **Animation:** 15 hours (simulation flows)
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 8 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements
*   Simulation environment templates
*   Variable indicator graphics
*   Impact visualization elements
*   "What If" scenario library

## Reusable Template Elements
*   Opening "simulation loading" sequence
*   Event trigger animations
*   Data overlay graphics
*   Outcome prediction displays

## Team Composition Needed
*   **Scenario Designer/Researcher**
*   **Writer**
*   **3D/Isometric illustrator**
*   **Motion graphics animator**
*   **Voiceover**
