# 5. Target Niche

## Primary Subject Matter

*   Business failures
*   Career downfalls
*   Product flops

## Audience Psychographics

*   Entrepreneurs wanting to avoid mistakes
*   Business students looking for case studies
*   Schadenfreude enjoyers
*   Documentary enthusiasts

## Differentiation Angle

*   **Approach:** Clinical, systematic approach (vs. dramatic storytelling).
*   **Emphasis:** Emphasize patterns and lessons, not just entertainment.
