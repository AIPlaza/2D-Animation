# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 40-60 hours

*   **Research:** 15 hours (critical for credibility)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 8 hours
*   **Storyboarding:** 4 hours
*   **Illustration:** 10 hours (reusable elements)
*   **Animation:** 8 hours
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 8 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements

*   Evidence board template (reusable)
*   Character base designs (investigator + generic "subject" variations)
*   Graph/chart templates
*   Transition effects library

## Reusable Template Elements

*   Opening sequence (30 seconds)
*   Section dividers ("The Evidence", "The Cause", etc.)
*   Evidence tag animation
*   Timeline scrubber
*   End card with "What We Learned" summary

## Team Composition Needed

*   **Solo Creator:** 1 person (if using templates, possible)
*   **Optimal Team:**
    *   Researcher/Writer
    *   Illustrator
    *   Video Editor
    *   Voiceover artist (or AI)
