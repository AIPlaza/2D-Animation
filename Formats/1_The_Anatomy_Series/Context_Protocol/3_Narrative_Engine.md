# 3. Narrative Engine

## Story Structure: Investigation Framework

*   **The Scene (0:00-1:30):** Present the "body" (failed company, ruined career, etc.)
*   **The Autopsy (1:30-8:00):** Systematic breakdown of contributing factors
*   **The Diagnosis (8:00-10:00):** Root cause identification
*   **The Lessons (10:00-12:00):** What we can learn + prevention

## Episode Arc Pattern

*   Cold open with shocking outcome
*   Flashback to peak moment
*   Chronological breakdown of decay
*   Pattern recognition across cases

## Series Arc Potential

*   **Season 1:** Tech Companies
*   **Season 2:** Career Implosions
*   **Season 3:** Product Failures

## Character Role Definitions

*   **The Investigator (narrator avatar):** Detached, analytical, occasionally sardonic
*   **The Subject (person/company):** Shown through archival visuals
*   **The Witnesses (supporting characters):** Quote sources, experts
