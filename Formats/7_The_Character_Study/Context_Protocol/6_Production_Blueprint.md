# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 65-85 hours
*   **Research:** 30 hours (deep profile needed)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 15 hours (nuance is hard)
*   **Storyboarding:** 6 hours
*   **Illustration:** 18 hours (detailed portraits)
*   **Animation:** 12 hours
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 8 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements
*   High-quality portrait illustrations
*   Symbolic imagery library
*   Psychological concept visualizations
*   Timeline templates

## Reusable Template Elements
*   Portrait gallery frame
*   Thought visualization bubbles
*   Timeline scrubber
*   "Public vs Private" split screen

## Team Composition Needed
*   **Psychologically-minded researcher**
*   **Empathetic writer**
*   **Portrait illustrator**
*   **Voiceover with emotional range**
