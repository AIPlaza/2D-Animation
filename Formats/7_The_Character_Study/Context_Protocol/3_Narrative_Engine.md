# 3. Narrative Engine

## Story Structure: Psychological Profiling
*   **The Persona (0:00-2:00):** Public image
*   **The Origin (2:00-5:00):** Formative experiences
*   **The Pattern (5:00-10:00):** Behavioral analysis
*   **The Contradiction (10:00-12:00):** Where image breaks
*   **The Legacy (12:00-15:00):** Long-term impact

## Episode Arc Pattern
*   Start with public perception
*   Reveal private reality
*   Show the gap between
*   Analyze the why

## Series Arc Potential
*   "Misunderstood Villains"
*   "Fallen Heroes"
*   "The Genius and the Madness"

## Character Role Definitions
*   **The Subject (protagonist):** Complex, multifaceted
*   **The Narrator (analyst):** Empathetic but critical
*   **The Context (supporting cast):** People around subject
