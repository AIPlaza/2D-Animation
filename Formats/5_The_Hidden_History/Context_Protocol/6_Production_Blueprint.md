# 6. Production Blueprint

## Estimated Production Time Per Episode: 55-70 hours
*   **Research:** 25 hours (sourcing is critical)
*   **Scriptwriting:** 10 hours
*   **Storyboarding:** 5 hours
*   **Illustration/Photo sourcing:** 12 hours
*   **Animation:** 10 hours
*   **Voiceover:** 2 hours
*   **Editing:** 8 hours
*   **Thumbnail:** 2 hours

## Key Asset Requirements
*   Historical photo library access
*   Document animation templates
*   Period-appropriate design elements
*   Map templates

## Reusable Template Elements
*   File folder opening
*   Document zoom effects
*   Historical newspaper headlines
*   "Then vs Now" comparison cards

## Team Composition Needed
*   **Historian/Researcher**
*   **Writer**
*   **Animator (motion graphics focus)**
*   **Voiceover**
